# Sample data for testing

Use **`sample-timeseries.csv`** to test the Data Management page:

1. Open the app → **Data** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Choose file** and select `sample-timeseries.csv`.
3. After upload, it appears in the table. Click a row to preview, and **Set active** to use it for training and forecasting.

The CSV has 30 rows: `date` and `value` (simple time series).
